1. Compose dialog — new "Track" toggle + CC/BCC reveal

Default state — CC/BCC hidden behind a small link to keep the dialog clean:

Expanded state — clicking "+ Cc · Bcc" reveals two extra inputs (comma-separated emails, validated inline):

Email Sarah Chen

Sarah Chen · sarah@acme.com

Cc recipients see each other's addresses and can trigger opens/clicks (counted under the same activity). Bcc recipients are blind to other recipients; their opens/clicks also roll up under the same activity. The activity-detail page lists Cc/Bcc addresses separately so you know who was included.
