9 Invitation lifecycle

10 Test coverage
✓Tenancy per endpoint — a user in org B can't read/touch org A's members, invites, or records (404/403).
✓Role gating — a member is blocked from invite / role-change / remove / settings (403).
✓Member write-own — can edit/delete only owned records; blocked on others' (403).
✓Last-owner invariant — the final owner can't be demoted, removed, or leave.
✓Invitations — expired/invalid token rejected; accept joins once (no double-join).
✓E2E (Playwright) — invite → accept → switch org.
