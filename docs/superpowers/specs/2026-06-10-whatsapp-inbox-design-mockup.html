Sending goes through a WhatsAppProvider interface: SandboxWhatsAppProvider (build + test now) ↔ Dialog360WhatsAppProvider (flip live via config) — exactly like buildEmailProvider. Per-org keys live in the encrypted OrgIntegrationsRepo; the AI draft reuses @dealflow/ai.

🔁 Flows
📥 Inbound
customer sends→360dialog→webhook POST→verify HMAC sig→normalize phone → match contact→store msg (contactId or null)→200 fast
📤 Outbound
rep types / uses AI draft→24h-window check→provider.send→store out msg (sent)→status webhook → delivered/read ✓✓
✨ AI draft reply
click "Draft reply"→thread context + SEA tone (BM/Manglish/EN)→@dealflow/ai→suggestion in composer→rep edits + sends
